Policy Guidelines

Rules and best practices to ensure a secure, transparent, and broker-free house-hunting experience for all Ugandans.

Guidelines for Customers

  • Never Pay Before Inspection:

    Do not send money (mobile money or cash) as "booking fees" before physically visiting the location and inspecting the room.

  • Say No to Broker Fees:

    Our platform is 100% free of brokers. If any person claiming to be a landlord demands "viewing fees" or agent commission, refuse and report them immediately.

  • Verify Landlord Identity:

    When inspecting the house, request to meet the property manager or caretaker directly at the compound to confirm legitimacy.

Regulations for Landlords

  • Accurate & Real Images:

    You must upload three distinct, real images showing the front view, the interior bedroom view, and any other layout (e.g. bathroom). Fake or stock photos will result in account suspension.

  • Honest Pricing:

    Prices listed must be correct monthly rents in UGX. Do not add hidden agent surcharges or inflate the cost upon meeting the tenant.

  • Prompt Availability Updates:

    As soon as your room is rented out/taken, you must log into your dashboard and toggle its status to "Taken" to remove it from public search results.

Strict Anti-Broker/Blocker Policy

House brokers (blockers) are strictly prohibited from posing as landlords, listing properties, or soliciting fees of any kind on Dream House Uganda. Our database is routinely inspected, and suspicious listings will be permanently flagged.
